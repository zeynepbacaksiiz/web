Download and Find on the Map

Download BinBin app from the App Store and Google Play Store to get started. Find the closest BinBin to you by using the map in the application.

Istanbul, Izmir, Konya, Kayseri, Samsun, Eskisehir, Antalya, Kocaeli, Bursa, Adana, Gaziantep, Trabzon, Diyarbakir and Sakarya are among the cities we currently serve. The current pricing is TRY1.99 at opening and TRY1.49 per minute. Do not forget to follow us on our social media accounts for our newly opened service areas!

Scan and Ride

By scanning the QR code on BinBin by your camera, you can unlock it and get it ready to ride. Don't forget to use your protective equipment for safety and speed up with your foot and push on the gas!

Park and Take a Picture

To end your ride, park in a suitable area on the map, lock BinBin in a safe place and take a picture. You can see the details of your ride on the screen that appears at the end of the ride.
